Laboratory Rules and Regulations

       To avoid embarrassment and maintain camaraderie with your friends and superiors at our laboratories, please observe the following:

  1.   Maintain silence, proper decorum, and discipline inside the laboratory. Mobile phones, walkmans, and other personal equipment must be switched off.
  2.   Games are not allowed inside the lab, including computer-related games, card games, and others that may disturb the lab.
  3.   Internet usage is only allowed with the instructor's permission. Downloading and installing software is strictly prohibited.
  4.   Accessing inappropriate or non-course-related websites is strictly prohibited.
  5.   Tampering with computer files or settings is a major offense.
  6.   Computer usage is limited to 15 minutes when others are waiting.
  8.   Observe proper decorum:
    •   a. Enter the lab only when the instructor is present.
    •   b. Deposit bags at the counter.
    •   c. Follow the seating arrangement.
    •   d. Close all programs at the end of class.
    •   e. Return chairs to their proper places.
  9.   Eating, drinking, smoking, and vandalism are prohibited.
  10.   Continuous disturbances will result in removal from the lab.
  11.   Hostile or threatening behavior will not be tolerated.
  12.   Severe offenses will be reported to security.
  13.   Report technical issues to lab personnel immediately.

Disciplinary Action

  • First Offense: - The Head or the Dean or OIC recommends to the Guidance Center for a suspension from classes for each offender.
  • Second & Subsequent Offenses: - A recommendation for a heavier sanction will be endorsed to the Guidance Center.
